FREEDOM THE RHYTHM

First Sight

In 2015, a community called Frhythm Family was born in Lampung, with the main goal being dance covers. With an initial formation of 7 people, they began to make their mark in the local entertainment world. Creativity and enthusiasm become an attraction, forming a solid and enthusiastic community.

Key Value

Core values maintain a consistent community and represent the pursuit of value and discipline. In expressing a dance, they should always be considered and be creative in various experience dance genre.

Achievement

We often win events in every competition, either 1st, 2nd or 3rd place in Bandar Lampung City. We also collaborate with sponsors and get support from the members' parents which makes us even more trustworthy.
